About Missy

A Story of Hope & Giving Back

At just 10 years old, Missy Schwartz Rodriguez underwent an experimental liver transplant — a moment that would change the course of her life forever. Now 52, Missy marks every anniversary of that lifesaving gift by pouring her heart into others.

A private college advisor based in Short Hills, NJ, Missy is uniquely positioned to inspire the next generation. She founded the Missy's Miracle Scholarship Fund 12 years ago — a true labor of love — and has built Missy's Miracle Clubs at Millburn High School and at her own alma mater, Montville Township High School. The mission extends to college campuses too, with awareness events at Indiana University through ZBT and the University of Arizona through AEPI.

Her passion has been recognized at every level — from the pages of Vicinity Magazine to the Prudential Center jumbotron, from the Transplant Games of America to the 2024 Rose Bowl Parade. But to Missy, the real miracle is simply the chance to give back.

"I'm so proud of the work I am doing, and I'm thrilled to know that I am making a difference in people's lives through organ and tissue donation advocacy."

Our Mission

Why We Do This

"Educate and support pre and post transplant recipients and donor families."

Missy's Miracle provides scholarships to high school students across New Jersey whose lives have been touched by organ and tissue donation — honoring every donor who makes miracles possible.

Recognition

Honored & Recognized

2024 Rose Bowl Parade

Missy was selected as a Rider on the Donate Life float — "Woven Together: The Dance of Life" — representing liver transplant recipients at the iconic Rose Bowl Parade in Pasadena, California.

Donate Life Float Rider

Featured as Missy Rodriguez — Rider, Liver Recipient in the official 2024 Rose Bowl "Woven Together" program, alongside donors, recipients, and advocates from across the country.

Prudential Center

Missy's Miracle Scholarship Fund was featured on the Prudential Center jumbotron — a proud moment of visibility for organ donation awareness in front of thousands of fans.

Vicinity Magazine — Local Heroes

Featured in Vicinity Magazine's "Local Heroes" issue, Missy's story of transformation — from transplant recipient to community advocate — inspired readers across New Jersey.

Community 5K

Missy regularly participates in community 5K races to raise awareness for organ and tissue donation — running side by side with supporters every step of the way.

Transplant Games of America

Multiple gold medals as a tennis player representing Team Liberty at the Transplant Games of America — competing proudly as a transplant recipient and life advocate.

Mayor & Millburn Township Proclamation

Officially recognized by the Mayor and Millburn Township with a formal proclamation honoring Missy's outstanding philanthropic contributions to the community through organ and tissue donation advocacy.

Temple B'Nai Jeshurun

Featured as a panelist at the Women's Association's "Be the Change! Women of TBJ Making a Difference in Our World" event — recognized among inspiring women changing the world for the better.

Activities & Events

Ways We Come Together

🏈 Annual Turkey Bowl

Co-ed flag football every Thanksgiving morning at Millburn High School. Teams of 6–8 players, $30 per player. A beloved tradition that brings the whole community together while raising scholarship funds.

🎳 Back to School Bowling Bash

Annual 3v3 bowling tournament at Hanover Lanes to kick off the school year. $30 per person, prizes, and a whole lot of fun — all for a great cause.

🍪 College Cookie Fundraiser

Custom college-logo cookies (Oreos & sugar cookies) sold in 6-packs for $30. Nut-free, delicious, and perfect for college-bound students — proceeds go directly to scholarships.

🌯 Chipotle Fundraiser Nights

Community dining fundraisers where 25% of sales are donated to Missy's Miracle. Show the flyer, enjoy a burrito, and help change a life.

🎒 Back to School Pickup

Annual gear giveaway featuring hand sanitizers, Touchless Point keychains, and Chick-fil-A sandwiches. A $15 suggested contribution supports the scholarship fund.

👕 Missy's Miracle Merchandise

Official Missy's Miracle hoodies and gear — wear your support proudly. Text 917-913-6731 for available colors and sizes. All proceeds benefit the scholarship fund.
